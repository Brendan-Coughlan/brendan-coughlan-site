Interview Coach

Interview Coach is an AI-powered web application developed by a team of three for CSC 430: Software Engineering. The platform helps job seekers prepare for interviews by offering personalized mock sessions, resume-based question generation, and instant AI feedback. Built with Next.js, FastAPI, and the OpenAI API, it allows users to practice industry-specific questions, refine their responses, and track progress over time.
